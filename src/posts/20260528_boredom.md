title: driving in silence
tags: thoughts
date: 2026-05-28
description: trying to reclaim my attention span
---

in modern society, no one need be bored. we are surrounded by technology and entertainment. where listening to music was once intentional, it now hums beneath the current of our lives. working, driving, waiting, showering, sleeping; we listen and scroll and are not bored.

but boredom had a purpose. boredom was productive.

it gave us time to think, to contemplate who we are, what we want. it inspired us, fueled our creativity, allowed us to reflect. we pursued our curiosity and discovered who we are. 

my days, weeks, months are filled, and i’m exhausted. yet there is always so much to do. 

 sometimes, i drive in silence.
